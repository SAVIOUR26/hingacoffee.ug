HINGA COFFEE –COMPANY PROFILE
1. Company Overview
Hinga Coffee is a proudly Ugandan-owned coffee company focused on producing, processing, and exporting premium quality coffee to local and international markets. The company is rooted in Uganda’s rich agricultural heritage and leverages modern processing techniques to deliver consistent, high-grade Arabica and Robusta coffee products. 
Hinga
Hinga’s operations are built on a vertically integrated value chain, ensuring quality control from cultivation to final delivery. The company collaborates with coffee farmers across Uganda to produce coffee that reflects the country’s unique soil, altitude, and climate advantages.

Head Office: Namanve Industrial Park, Mukono, Uganda

Uganda is among Africa’s major coffee producers, benefiting from ideal climatic conditions for both Robusta and Arabica varieties. Companies like Hinga play an important role in increasing value addition locally rather than exporting only raw beans.
